Three Cute Facts About Rabbits

Read time: 5 minutes

Bunnies are tiny athletes. They can jump as high as 90cm in one leap. Beware of their binkkies!

Bunnies are so cute!

Rabbits can give birth up to 14 babies in a single litter. Baby rabbits are called kittens.

Rabbits' teeth never stop growing! Make sure to give them lots of hay or wood so that they can file their teeth
